About the Museum

Salem Museum presents the rich cultural heritage of Tamil Nadu with artifacts, sculptures, and history.
Government Museum Salem is functioning at Sarada College Road, Fairlands Salem- 636 016 from the year 2007 onwards.
The Salem Government Museum was formed by the then District Collector Thiru.A.M.Swaminathan,I.A.S., with the support of the Local Artist Group called Contemporary Writers and Painters Association.
The Museum was opened to the public in the year 1976. From the year 1976 to 1979 it was functioning under the District Administration.
Later in the year 1979 it came under the Department of Museums.
From 1979 to 1996 Govt.Museum Salem was functioning at the Three Bungalow .
From 1996 to 2007 January it was functioning at the Court building. This was the first District Museum created by the people,
the people and formed the basis for the creation of new District Museums.
